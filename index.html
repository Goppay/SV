🇬🇭 About GhanaOpinionPay

GhanaOpinionPay is Ghana's premier paid survey and opinion platform — built by Ghanaians, for Ghanaians. We connect everyday citizens with brands, researchers, and organisations who need honest feedback on the products and services they deliver.

Our Mission

To empower every Ghanaian with the opportunity to earn real money simply by sharing their voice. Whether you're a student, a professional, a trader, or a homemaker — your opinion has value, and we make sure you get paid for it.

What We Offer

  • Paid surveys on topics ranging from banking to food, health, elections, and more
  • Sponsored video views from Ghanaian and African brands
  • Daily polls and opinion questions
  • A referral programme that rewards you for bringing in others
  • Fast and reliable GHS payouts via Mobile Money and bank transfer

Our Values

  • Transparency — We are honest about how much you earn and when you get paid
  • Integrity — We never sell your personal data without consent
  • Inclusion — We serve all 16 regions of Ghana across all tiers
  • Accountability — Every payment is tracked and verified by our admin team

📜 Terms of Service

Last updated: January 2025

1. Eligibility

GhanaOpinionPay is open to Ghanaian residents aged 18 and above. By registering, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old and that the information you provide is accurate and truthful.

2. Account Registration

Each person may hold only one account. Creating multiple accounts to earn additional rewards is strictly prohibited and will result in permanent banning of all associated accounts. Your registered Mobile Money number is permanent and cannot be changed.

3. Earning & Surveys

You must answer all survey questions honestly and thoughtfully. Providing random, inconsistent, or dishonest answers to complete surveys faster may result in earnings being withheld and your account being suspended. GhanaOpinionPay reserves the right to review survey responses for quality.

4. Withdrawals

Withdrawals are processed instantly. Minimum withdrawal amounts apply per tier. A processing fee applies from the second withdrawal onwards (or on every withdrawal for Free tier accounts). GhanaOpinionPay is not responsible for delays caused by Mobile Money networks or banks.

GhanaOpinionPay reserves the right to suspend or permanently terminate any account found to be in violation of these terms, including but not limited to: fraudulent activity, multiple accounts, fake survey responses, abuse of the referral programme, or any conduct deemed harmful to the platform.

6. Changes to Terms

GhanaOpinionPay may update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms. Major changes will be communicated to members via in-platform notifications.

7. Limitation of Liability

GhanaOpinionPay provides the platform on an "as is" basis. We are not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from your use of the platform. Our total liability to any member shall not exceed the total earnings accumulated in their account.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register: full name, email address, Mobile Money number, region, age, and gender. We also collect survey responses, activity data (surveys completed, videos watched, polls answered), and transaction records.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and manage your GhanaOpinionPay account
  • To process your earnings and withdrawals
  • To match you with relevant surveys and content
  • To detect and prevent fraud and multiple accounts
  • To communicate platform updates and notifications
  • To provide anonymised, aggregated research data to clients

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Survey responses may be shared with research clients in aggregated, anonymised form only — your name, phone number, and personal details are never disclosed. We may share information with payment processors (such as Paystack) solely to facilitate withdrawals.

4. Data Storage

Your account data is currently stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means your data remains on your device. GhanaOpinionPay does not operate a central server that stores your personal information at this time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at Ghanaopinionpay@gmail.com. Account deletion requests will be processed within 7 business days.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no online platform can guarantee 100% security. Please use a strong, unique password for your account and do not share your login details with anyone.

7. Contact

Privacy concerns? Email us at Ghanaopinionpay@gmail.com.
