Questions & Answers

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about GhanaOpinionPay.

Sign-up is free. A one-time membership fee applies: GH₵ 60 (Bronze), GH₵ 100 (Silver), or GH₵ 150 (Gold), paid via Mobile Money prompt sent to your registered number after sign-up. You also receive a GH₵ 5 welcome bonus on activation.
All withdrawals pass through three phases: Pending → Processing → Complete. The full process takes at least 2 business days. You will receive a notification at each phase change. Payments are processed on Mondays and Thursdays.
After completing sign-up, a Mobile Money payment prompt is automatically sent to your registered phone number. Approve it on your phone to activate your account. Works on MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash (formerly Vodafone Cash), and AirtelTigo Money.
GhanaOpinionPay is open to Ghanaian residents aged 18 and above with an active Mobile Money account. Ghana Card or voter ID required for verification before your first cashout. One account per household.
Active members typically earn between GH₵ 40 and GH₵ 150 per month. Gold-tier members with active referrals can earn significantly more. Maximum monthly withdrawal limit: GH₵ 2,000.
Prompts typically arrive within 2 minutes. If it doesn't arrive, manually send the registration fee to 0203910730 and email support@ghanaopinionpay.com with your name and number for manual activation.
